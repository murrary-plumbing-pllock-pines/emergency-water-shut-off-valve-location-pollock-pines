## Common Locations of Emergency Water Shut Off Valves in Pollock Pines
- **Inside the home:** In homes with crawl spaces or basements, the valve is often located near the water meter or at the point where the main water line enters the building.
- **Outside the home:** In some cases, especially for homes with slab foundations, the shut off valve may be located outside, typically near the meter or in a utility box by the property line.

---

## How to Find Your Emergency Water Shut Off Valve
1. **Locate the water meter:** In many homes, the shut off valve is near or connected to the water meter. The meter is usually located in a utility box or near the front of the property.
2. **Check the foundation or crawl space:** For homes with a crawl space, check along the wall where the water supply enters. The shut off valve will often be positioned there.
3. **Look for outdoor valves:** If you live in an area with a slab foundation, your valve may be located outside near the water meter or property line.
