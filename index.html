Emergency Water Shut-Off Valve
The valve that controls the water supply to a home and can be used to stop water flow during an emergency.
Crawl Space
A small area beneath a home, often where plumbing systems and shut-off valves are located, particularly in regions with cold climates.
Pipe Freeze
When water inside a pipe freezes due to low temperatures, causing it to expand and potentially rupture the pipe.
Shut-Off Mechanism
A method, either manual or automatic, that allows homeowners to quickly stop the water supply in case of a plumbing emergency.
